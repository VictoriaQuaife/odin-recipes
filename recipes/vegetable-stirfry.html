Vegetable Stir Fry

A quick easy recipe but full of flavour. Colourful vegetables sauteed in a quick sweet and savory sauce, can be served with noodles or rice.

Stir fires are perfect when you have vegetables in the fridge that need using up, feel free to swap out any of the vegetables mentioned below.

Ingredients

Vegetable Stir Fry:

  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • 1 pack of long stemmed broccoli florets
  • 1 pack of baby corn
  • 100g mushrooms, sliced or quartered
  • 1 whole pepper (red, yellow or orange), seeded and sliced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • 2 tsp ginger, minced
Stir Fry sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth or vegetable broth for vegtarians
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp hot sauce (optional)

Method

  1. In a large wok, over medium heat, heat the oil, add the garlic and ginger and heat for 10 seconds
  2. Add the vegetables and fry until tender, approx 3 minutes
  3. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients for the stir fry sauce
  4. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir
  5. Turn heat down to medium/low and cook for 3-4 minutes, until sauce thickens and vegeables are desired tenderness.
