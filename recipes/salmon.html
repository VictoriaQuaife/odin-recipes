Baked Salmon

Try out this wonderfully delicious, easy baked salmon recipe. Perfect for lunch or dinner, packed full of omega-3.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • chopped herbs such as rosemary
  • lemon slices from 2 lemons
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4.
  2. Place foil on a baking tray with enough excess to cover salmon (optional).
  3. Place half of lemon slices and herbs on foil.
  4. Place salmon fillets on top of lemon and herbs.
  5. Rub oil over salmon or place small square of butter on each fillet.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Place remaining lemon slices and herbs on top of fillets.
  8. Fold foil into parcel so there are no openings for fillets to be tender or cook without foil for flesh to be roasted slightly.
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes until just opague and easily flaked with a fork.
  10. Serve with fresh vegetables such as asparagus or long-stemmed broccoli- Enjoy!
